[17/11/2017] Organic sample based instrument

I've been thinking on some advice given my Deru "Turn organic sounds into electronic and electronic into organic". Based on this I've been creating an instrument from two classical instruments sounds, a single 1 second sample of a Soprano singing a latin phrase and a single 1 second sample of a Violin. Part of a new piece of music I'm working on.
